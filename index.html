The World of 2075: The Rise of Root

The year 2075 marks a pivotal moment in human history as, after half a century of growing government surveillance and data control, citizens of the world collectively rejected centralised authority and the surveillance state. VPNs, once optional, became as essential as running water. Public trust in governments and institutions eroded, banking systems were replaced with decentralised currencies, and communities regained their autonomy to combat global challenges. This new era of independence led to innovation and creation, including a tool to fight global warming as well as a new educational system, Root.

Root was developed as a decentralised educational platform, independently founded and operated outside government control. In this new world, knowledge spreads through a global network where anyone can share verified information, allowing learning to evolve beyond traditional structures. Root has fully replaced conventional schools and universities as the primary source of education, ensuring that information is delivered in a factual and unbiased manner, free from governmental influence.
